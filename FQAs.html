Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the duration of the courses?
Courses run from 3 to 9 months depending on the chosen specialization. Each course is tailored to ensure full understanding and practical implementation.
2. Who issues the certificates?
Certificates are offered by New Gen Innovators Network, authorized by Professor BlackLogic—the lecturer and founder of the platform.
3. How does enrollment work?
70% of our programs are online. Some programs include practical assessments and final testing. This ensures students are ready for real-world tech industry demands.
4. Who is eligible for support?
We encourage all young candidates between the ages of 18 to 35 to embark on this journey of tech education and skill development.
5. What is the vision of New Gen Innovative Network?
Our purpose is to equip youth with essential tech skills. We believe in teamwork and collaboration to change South Africa’s economy through AI, ML, and software engineering.
